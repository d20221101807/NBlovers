New Balance 1906

Paying homage to the year New Balance was founded, the 1906 offers a dynamic blend of retro aesthetics and high-performance technology. Featuring mesh and leather uppers with aggressive overlays, the 1906 is equipped with the same N-ERGY cushioning as the 2002R. Its sporty design pairs well with both activewear and urban-inspired outfits. Style Highlights: Bold, sporty design with heritage appeal. Perfect For: Athleisure looks, running errands, and standing out with a retro-inspired vibe.
